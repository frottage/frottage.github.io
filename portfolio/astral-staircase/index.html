Astral Staircase

Water, fire and light

This night, as I was walking home, I passed upwards through the forest. Upon entering the forest, my vibrational energy started increasing.

It’s raining but I’m warm. I stopped, looking up the path. Behind me is single street lamp. Large rain drops dripped from the branches above. I exhaled. My warm breath went out in front of me in a cloud, illuminated by the street lamp behind me, and the large rain drops glistened in the background.

I’m mixing water, fire and light.

I am reminded of the Gospel of Philip.
