A girl believes she must prepare for her photo
to be taken. So she wears a low cut, frilly
garment, and splays her fiery red hair, which
she put great effort into, and is so proud of.
She poses for the viewer so they can see.

Ceramic Mannequin

In this surreal artwork, flowers confidently stand from the sawn-off head of a ceramic mannequin. Water endlessly surfaces from the neck and quietly overflows from the lip. The arms and hands delicately embrace each other with attention towards the beholder in a sign of defencelessness and trust.
