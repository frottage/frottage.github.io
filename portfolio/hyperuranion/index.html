Aeons

The superstructure of the celestial system, the celestial world of Aeons, is here developed in the most complicated way.

These Aeons belong to the purely ideal, noumenal, intelligible, or supersensible world; they are immaterial, they are hypostatic ideas.

Together with the source from which they emanate they form the Pleroma.

The transition from the immaterial to the material, from the noumenal to the sensible, is brought about by a flaw, or a passion, or a sin, in the female Aeon Sophia.
