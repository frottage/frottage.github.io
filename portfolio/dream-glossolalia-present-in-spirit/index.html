Dream: Glossolalia, Present in Spirit

My dream

At 5am this morning I had a dream of perhaps 1000 people sitting on the ground within a building, all facing the same direction. I myself was sitting between two friends of mine, Simon and Megan. Simon asked me for a red fruit burst - Strawberry Flavour, which I gladly handed to him. I then began speaking in tongues. I also believe I felt bubbles of living water through my light body. Also one such bubble came through the top of my head, through my ear and then down to my heart.
